Privacy Policy

Kitchen Ink

Last updated: April 2026

Kitchen Ink is a personal recipe management app. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Who We Are

Kitchen Ink is operated by an individual developer of Mexican nationality. For legal purposes, this service is operated under Mexican jurisdiction. For privacy inquiries, contact us at the email address listed at the bottom of this document.

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly

  • Account information: email address and display name when you create an account
  • Recipe content: recipe titles, ingredients, instructions, and notes you enter
  • Photos: food photos you upload or generate using our AI tools
  • Household information: names and ages of household members you add to Mi Hogar (optional)
  • Group content: messages and recipes shared within family groups

Information collected automatically

  • Usage data: which features you use within the app (collected anonymously for product improvement)
  • Device information: device type and operating system for compatibility purposes

Information we do NOT collect

  • Location data — we never request or store your location
  • Payment information — we do not process payments directly
  • Contacts or address book
  • Biometric data

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the Kitchen Ink app and its features
  • To enable recipe sharing within your family groups
  • To generate AI-powered recipe photos (using Google Gemini) and scan recipes (using Anthropic Claude)
  • To calculate nutritional estimates based on ingredients you enter
  • To improve the app based on anonymous usage patterns
  • To respond to your support requests

4. Third-Party Services

Kitchen Ink uses the following third-party services to operate:

  • Google Firebase: database storage, authentication, and cloud functions. Your data is stored on Google's secure servers. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Google Gemini API: AI image generation for recipe photos. Images you generate are processed by Google. Google Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic Claude API: AI recipe scanning and meal planning features. Text content processed through this service is subject to Anthropic's policies. Anthropic Privacy Policy

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We do not share your personal information with advertisers or third parties for marketing purposes.

We may share data only in these circumstances:

  • With family group members you explicitly invite — recipes and messages you share in a group are visible to all group members
  • With third-party services listed above, strictly to operate app features
  • If required by law or to protect legal rights

6. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us. Upon deletion request, we will remove your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

7. Children's Privacy

Kitchen Ink is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately and we will take steps to remove that information.

For users between 13 and 17, we recommend parental guidance when using the app.

8. Your Rights

Regardless of your location, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal data we collect and how it is used
  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate personal data
  • Delete your personal data
  • Opt out of the sale of personal data (we do not sell data)

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email address below.

9. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures including Firebase's built-in security, encrypted data transmission (HTTPS), and authentication tokens. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password for your account.

10. AI-Generated Content Disclaimer

Kitchen Ink uses artificial intelligence to assist with recipe scanning, meal planning, and photo generation. AI-generated content may contain errors or inaccuracies. Nutritional information provided by the app is an estimate and should not be used as a substitute for professional nutritional or medical advice.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this document. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact

Kitchen Ink
Operated under Mexican jurisdiction
Email: kitcheninkapp@gmail.com

Política de Privacidad

Kitchen Ink

Última actualización: Abril 2026

Kitchen Ink es una app personal para gestionar recetas. Estamos comprometidos a proteger tu privacidad. Esta política explica qué información recopilamos, cómo la usamos y tus derechos sobre tus datos.

1. Quiénes somos

Kitchen Ink es operado por un desarrollador individual de nacionalidad mexicana. Para efectos legales, este servicio opera bajo jurisdicción mexicana. Para consultas de privacidad, contáctanos en el correo electrónico indicado al final de este documento.

2. Información que recopilamos

Información que proporcionas directamente

  • Información de cuenta: correo electrónico y nombre de usuario al crear tu cuenta
  • Contenido de recetas: títulos, ingredientes, instrucciones y notas que ingresas
  • Fotos: fotos de alimentos que subes o generas con nuestras herramientas de IA
  • Información del hogar: nombres y edades de los miembros que agregas a Mi Hogar (opcional)
  • Contenido de grupos: mensajes y recetas compartidas dentro de grupos familiares

Información recopilada automáticamente

  • Datos de uso: qué funciones utilizas dentro de la app (recopilados de forma anónima para mejorar el producto)
  • Información del dispositivo: tipo de dispositivo y sistema operativo para compatibilidad

Información que NO recopilamos

  • Datos de ubicación — nunca solicitamos ni almacenamos tu ubicación
  • Información de pago — no procesamos pagos directamente
  • Contactos o libreta de direcciones
  • Datos biométricos

3. Cómo usamos tu información

  • Para proporcionar y operar la app Kitchen Ink y sus funciones
  • Para habilitar el intercambio de recetas dentro de tus grupos familiares
  • Para generar fotos de recetas con IA (usando Google Gemini) y escanear recetas (usando Anthropic Claude)
  • Para calcular estimaciones nutricionales basadas en los ingredientes que ingresas
  • Para mejorar la app basándonos en patrones de uso anónimos
  • Para responder tus solicitudes de soporte

4. Servicios de terceros

Kitchen Ink utiliza los siguientes servicios de terceros para funcionar:

  • Google Firebase: almacenamiento de base de datos, autenticación y funciones en la nube. Tus datos se almacenan en los servidores seguros de Google.
  • Google Gemini API: generación de imágenes con IA para fotos de recetas.
  • Anthropic Claude API: escaneo de recetas con IA y funciones de planificación de comidas.

5. Compartir datos

No vendemos tus datos personales. No compartimos tu información personal con anunciantes ni terceros con fines comerciales.

6. Privacidad de menores

Kitchen Ink no está dirigida a menores de 13 años. No recopilamos intencionalmente información personal de niños menores de 13 años. Si crees que un menor de 13 años nos ha proporcionado información personal, contáctanos de inmediato.

7. Contenido generado por IA

Kitchen Ink utiliza inteligencia artificial para asistir con el escaneo de recetas, planificación de comidas y generación de fotos. El contenido generado por IA puede contener errores. La información nutricional proporcionada por la app es una estimación y no debe utilizarse como sustituto de consejo nutricional o médico profesional.
